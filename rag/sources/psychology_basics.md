## Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
CBT is a structured, time-limited form of psychotherapy focused on identifying and changing unhelpful thought patterns and behaviors.
It is commonly used for anxiety and depression.
A diagnosis or treatment plan can only be made by a qualified professional.

## Rumination
Rumination refers to repetitive, passive thinking about distressing experiences or emotions.
It can increase emotional distress when it becomes habitual.

## Emotional Numbness
Emotional numbness is the experience of feeling emotionally flat or disconnected.
It can occur during stress, burnout, depression, or trauma, but it is not a diagnosis by itself.
