## Inflation
Inflation refers to the general increase in prices over time, which reduces purchasing power.
Inflation rates vary by country and period and are influenced by economic policy, supply, and demand.

## Democracy
Democracy is a system of government in which power is vested in the people, typically through elected representatives.

## Artificial Intelligence
Artificial intelligence refers to computer systems designed to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and pattern recognition.
